Privacy Policy

Remaining  ·  Automata Studios  ·  Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Remaining is a home screen widget app that helps you visualize your time. We built this app with privacy as a core principle - it works entirely on your device without sending your personal data anywhere.

Short version: We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information. Everything stays on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavior data
  • Crash reports sent to external servers
  • Any form of telemetry

Data Stored Locally On Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device and never leaves it:

  • Birthday & life expectancy - used to calculate your "Life Remaining" widget. Optional.
  • Custom events - countdown names and dates you add manually.
  • Widget settings - colors, shapes, modes, reminder preferences.
  • App preferences - onboarding status, Pro purchase status.

This data is stored using Android's standard SharedPreferences system and may be backed up to your Google account via Android's Auto Backup feature, which is controlled by your Android settings.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions, all optional:

  • Calendar (READ_CALENDAR)
    Used to import your calendar events as countdown widgets. We only read event titles and dates. No calendar data is transmitted or stored outside your device.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS)
    Used to send you time reminders you configure in widget settings. We never send unsolicited notifications.
  • Internet (INTERNET)
    Required solely for in-app purchase processing via Google Play Billing. We do not make any other network requests.
  • Billing (com.android.vending.BILLING)
    Required to process the optional Pro upgrade through Google Play. All payment handling is done by Google Play - we never see your payment details.

In-App Purchases

Remaining offers an optional one-time Pro upgrade ($0.99). All purchases are processed securely by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment information. Purchase status is stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

The app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing - for processing the Pro upgrade. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play In-App Review - used to ask for a review after sufficient usage. No personal data is shared.

We do not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or any other third-party data collection services.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's security settings (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We recommend keeping your device's operating system and security patches up to date.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Going to Settings → Apps → Remaining → Storage → Clear Data on your Android device, or
  • Uninstalling the app

Either action permanently removes all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify users through an app update. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
