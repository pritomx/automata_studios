1Introduction

Muslim Companion ("the App", "we", "us") is developed and published by Automata Studios. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. We built Muslim Companion to work with as little personal data as possible — most of what the App stores never leaves your device.

By installing or using Muslim Companion, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you don't agree, please don't use the App.

2Information We Collect

Information stored only on your device

The following never reaches our servers — we don't operate a backend that stores this data at all:

  • 📍Saved location (latitude/longitude/city) — used to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction for your area.
  • 🧎Prayer logs & streaks — which prayers you've marked as offered, your current and best streaks, and earned badges.
  • 📖Quran reading progress — last read/listened position, reading streaks, and reciter/translation preferences.
  • ⚙️App settings — language, theme, calculation method, Hanafi/Shafi'i school, azaan and notification preferences.
  • 👤Display name — if you enter one during setup, it's used only to personalize greetings within the App.

Information collected by third-party services we use

Muslim Companion relies on a small number of external services to function (detailed in Section 5). These may independently collect limited technical data, such as:

  • 🌐Coordinates sent to the prayer-time API — your saved latitude/longitude is sent to Al Adhan's API each time prayer times or Qibla direction are calculated, so it can return accurate results for your location.
  • 📱Advertising & device identifiers — collected by Google AdMob for showing and measuring ads (see Section 6).
  • 💳Purchase information — handled entirely by Google Play Billing if you make an in-app purchase (see Section 7); we don't receive your payment details.

We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any account credentials — Muslim Companion doesn't require sign-up or login.

3How We Use Information

Whatever information is collected (by the App or its third-party services) is used only to:

  • 🕐Calculate accurate prayer times, Qibla direction, and the Hijri date for your location
  • 🔔Schedule local prayer-time and azaan notifications on your device
  • 🔥Track your prayer and Quran reading streaks and award badges
  • 📺Show relevant, non-intrusive ads that help keep the App free
  • 🛠️Diagnose and fix crashes or bugs if you contact us directly about one

We do not sell your information to anyone, and we don't use it for purposes unrelated to running the App.

6Advertising

Muslim Companion shows ads served through Google AdMob to help keep the App free. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as your advertising ID), approximate location, and app usage data to show and measure ads, and to limit how often you see the same ad. This data is governed by Google's Partner Sites policy and Google's Privacy Policy.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at the device level: on Android, go to Settings → Privacy → Ads, and turn off "Opt out of Ads Personalization" (wording varies by Android version). This doesn't reduce the number of ads you see, only how targeted they are.

7In-App Purchases

If Muslim Companion offers any paid features or an ad-free upgrade, purchases are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your card number, billing address, or other payment details — Google handles that under its own privacy and payments policies.

8Data Storage & Security

Almost everything Muslim Companion stores (settings, prayer logs, streaks, Quran progress) lives in local on-device storage and is never transmitted to us. We don't operate a server that stores your personal data. Because of this, we can't access, recover, or restore this data on your behalf if your device is lost, reset, or the App is uninstalled.

For the limited data that does pass through third-party services (Section 5), we choose providers that use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS) in transit.

9Data Retention & Deletion

Since your prayer logs, streaks, Quran progress, and settings are stored locally, you're always in full control of them:

  • 🗑️Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all locally stored data with it.
  • ⚙️You can clear individual settings or reset preferences directly within the App where such options are provided.
  • 📵Revoking location/notification permissions stops the App from collecting or using that data going forward.

Any advertising or purchase data held by Google is retained and deleted according to Google's own policies, not ours.

10Children's Privacy

Muslim Companion is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can address it.

11Your Choices & Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights such as accessing, correcting, or deleting your personal data, and objecting to certain processing. Because Muslim Companion stores almost all of your data locally on your own device, you can exercise most of these rights directly:

  • 📍Decline or revoke location access at any time in system settings
  • 🔔Disable prayer or azaan notifications from within the App or system settings
  • 📺Opt out of ad personalization via your device's privacy settings
  • 🗑️Delete all App data by uninstalling Muslim Companion

For anything not covered by the above, you can reach us using the contact details below.

12Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time as the App evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent revision. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of Muslim Companion after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
