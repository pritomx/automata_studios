1. Overview

DrutoPDF ("the App," "we," "us") is a PDF utility application that lets you convert, merge, split, compress, protect, and edit PDF files directly on your device. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using DrutoPDF, you agree to the practices described below.

Files processed on-device No file uploads to our servers Contains ads Offers in-app purchases

2. Data We Collect

DrutoPDF is designed to minimize data collection. We do not require account creation, and we do not collect the content of your documents. The categories of information involved in using the App are:

3. How Your Files Are Processed

This is the most important part of this policy: your PDF files never leave your device. All core operations — conversion, merging, splitting, compression, cropping, watermarking, page editing, password protection, and password recovery (bruteforce unlock) — are performed entirely locally on your device. DrutoPDF does not upload your documents to any server, cloud service, or third party for these operations.

This means: if a PDF is password-protected and you use the built-in recovery tool to attempt to unlock it, that process runs fully offline on your device — the file and any passwords tried are never transmitted anywhere.

4. Permissions We Request

Depending on your device and Android version, DrutoPDF may request the following permissions, used strictly for the stated purpose:

  • Storage / Media access — to let you select PDF files to work with and save the output files you create.
  • Notifications (if applicable) — to notify you when a long-running task (e.g. compression or bruteforce recovery) has finished.

We do not request access to your contacts, camera (unless you explicitly use an image-to-PDF feature involving the camera), microphone, or location.

5. Third-Party Services

DrutoPDF uses the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) — displays advertising within the App. May collect advertising identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • App store billing (Google Play Billing) — processes in-app purchases and subscriptions. We do not receive or store your payment card details; this is handled entirely by the platform's billing system.

6. Advertising

DrutoPDF displays ads served through Google Mobile Ads to support free use of the App. These ads may be personalized based on your advertising identifier and general usage patterns, subject to your device's ad privacy settings. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's system settings (e.g. "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android), or by purchasing the Pro version, which removes ads.

7. In-App Purchases

DrutoPDF offers optional Pro upgrades (lifetime and subscription options) that unlock additional features and remove ads. Purchases are processed entirely through your app store's billing system. We only receive confirmation of purchase status (e.g. "Pro unlocked") — we do not receive your payment information.

8. Data Storage & Retention

Preferences, recent file references, and purchase/unlock status are stored locally on your device using standard local storage mechanisms and are not synced to any server we control. Uninstalling the App removes this locally stored data. We do not operate servers that store your documents or personal files.

9. Children's Privacy

DrutoPDF is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can address it.

10. Security

Because your files are processed locally and never transmitted to our servers, the primary security consideration is your device itself. We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date and using a device lock screen for an added layer of protection over locally stored files.

11. Your Choices & Rights

  • You can clear the App's locally stored preferences and recent files at any time by clearing app data/storage in your device settings, or by uninstalling the App.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads via your device's ad settings.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Google Play account.
  • Depending on your region, you may have additional rights (e.g. under GDPR or CCPA) regarding data processed by our third-party providers (Google). Please refer to their respective privacy policies to exercise those rights.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable law. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
